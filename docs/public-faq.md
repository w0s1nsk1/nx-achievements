# Public FAQ

## Is this piracy-related?
No. NXAchievements does not include piracy tooling.

## Does this replace Nintendo Switch Online?
No. It does not replace or emulate Nintendo Switch Online.

## Does this scan memory?
Not in v0.1-dev.

## Does this support automatic unlocks?
Not in v0.1-dev.

## Does this require CFW?
The Switch viewer targets homebrew environments.

## Is online sync implemented?
No.

## Is this safe for stock consoles?
Stock console support is not a project goal.

## Why no Nintendo branding?
To avoid impersonation and copyright issues; the project uses original branding.
